Refund & Cancellation Policy

Please carefully review our refund and cancellation terms before purchasing or subscribing to any Tech Talk Titans services or products.

Last updated: June 26, 2025

1. General Policy

Tech Talk Titans maintains a strict no-refund and no-cancellation policy for all digital products, SaaS subscriptions, software services, development projects, consulting services, online courses, mentorship programs, cloud solutions, communication products, and technology services unless otherwise explicitly stated in writing.

2. No Refund Policy

All payments made to Tech Talk Titans are final and non-refundable.

Once a payment is successfully processed for any product or service, users shall not be entitled to request or claim any refund, reversal, reimbursement, or chargeback under normal circumstances.

3. SaaS Products & Digital Services

Payments for SaaS platforms, cloud applications, virtual communication systems, digital subscriptions, APIs, software licenses, and downloadable products are non-refundable due to the nature of instant digital access and infrastructure allocation.

4. Custom Development Projects

Payments made for custom software development, web development, mobile application development, UI/UX design, AI integrations, automation systems, cloud deployment, consulting, or technical implementation services are strictly non-refundable once project work, planning, resource allocation, or development activities have commenced.

5. Cancellation Policy

Users may choose to discontinue use of services at any time; however, cancellation of services does not create eligibility for refunds, partial refunds, prorated refunds, or reversal of previously paid charges.

6. Subscription Services

For recurring subscription-based services, users are responsible for managing cancellation requests before the next billing cycle. Once a subscription payment has been processed, the amount remains non-refundable.

7. Chargebacks & Payment Disputes

Unauthorized chargebacks, fraudulent disputes, or payment reversals initiated without prior communication may result in immediate suspension or permanent termination of services, accounts, and future business access.

8. Exceptional Circumstances

Tech Talk Titans reserves the sole and absolute right to evaluate exceptional cases, if any. Any consideration of refunds shall remain entirely at the discretion of Tech Talk Titans and shall not establish any obligation or precedent.

9. Acceptance of Policy

By purchasing, subscribing to, accessing, or using any Tech Talk Titans product or service, users acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agreed to this Refund & Cancellation Policy.
